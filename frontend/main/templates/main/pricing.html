Pricing

Your account will start with 30 credits. For every website you run it will go down by one every day. Websites with fewer than 50 users do not remove credits.

Buy 30 credits for $5 or 100 credits for $10. (so one website is $2-$3/mo).

NamelessMC software is free and open source. You can download and host it yourself for free. We donate a portion of our income to NamelessMC contributors.
